WEDP Loan

Aggar is one the eligible MFIs providing women Entrepreneurship

development project loans to women clients in Addis Ababa.

This loan is provided to female clients and the loan size is determined based

on the cash flow of the business.

The size of the loan can be up to Birr 900,000 and the loan term is 24 months.

We charge competitive interest rate and service charge.
